How It Works

Get started with Flitro in three simple steps

1

Configure Contexts

Open the main window and create your work contexts by adding apps, documents, browser tabs, and terminal sessions.

2

Choose Switch Mode

Select your preferred switching mode: Replace All for complete context switching, Additive to add to current setup, or Hybrid for smart switching.

3

Switch Instantly

Use the menu bar icon to select a context and switch your entire workspace with a single click. Your productivity just got supercharged!
